My dream is to mentor learners reach their potential!

A career coach helps you establish realistic goals, discover solutions to challenges you’re up against, develop action plans, build self-confidence, and gets you pumped up and motivated. You take charge of your career by changing it from what it is today to something you’ve always dreamed of—the ultimate goal.

But a career coach is not a therapist

A career coach is not a counselor or therapist. A career coach helps you develop proficiencies, whereas a career counselor helps their clients overcome deficiencies.
